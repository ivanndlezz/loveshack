# Pricing

## Base Prices (1-14 passengers)

| Duration | Price  |
| -------- | ------ |
| 2 hours  | $1,200 |
| 3 hours  | $1,800 |
| 4 hours  | $2,400 |
| 5 hours  | $3,000 |
| 6 hours  | $3,600 |
| 7 hours  | $4,200 |
| 8 hours  | $4,800 |

- **Rate:** $600 USD per hour
- **Minimum:** 2 hours

## Additional Passengers

- **Base coverage:** 1-14 passengers included
- **Extra passengers (15+):** $100 USD per person
- **Maximum:** 50 passengers

## Get My Boat Special Rate

- **Rate:** $500 USD per hour (instead of $600)
- **Minimum:** 2 hours
- **Fee:** 11.5% (charged to customer)

### Get My Boat Example Calculation

Trip: 3 hours, 23 passengers

```
Base calculation:
3 hours × $500 = $1,500
23 - 14 = 9 extra passengers
9 × $100 = $900
Total base: $1,500 + $900 = $2,400

With Get My Boat fee (11.5%):
$2,400 ÷ 0.885 = $2,712 (customer pays)
Fee: $311.88
Business receives: $2,400.12
```

## Extra Services

- **Extra Passenger:** $100 USD per person
- **Fishing License:** $22 USD per person (when applicable)
